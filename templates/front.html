About the familybook

The familybook is an homage to the ever so more popular book of faces (or: facebook). Contrary to its globally known and used cousin, the familybook is dedicated to serve only a small community, your family! I know, who would have thought... It is basically an attempt to invite your loved ones onto a platform where you can punish each other with all the cat and baby pictures that you can find.

In case of protest, you are encouraged to molest the administrator of this special community . Be warned though, there is a strong possibility, that these messages might grow old in the nether regions of this clan specific inbox. So, perseverance is advised.
